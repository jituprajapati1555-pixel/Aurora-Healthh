Comprehensive Medical Services, Under one Roof

Whether it’s a routine check‑up or a complex procedure, our multi‑specialty team provides coordinated care designed around you.

Clinical departments

Every department is led by experienced consultants and supported by trained nurses, technicians, and allied health professionals.

Cardiology & Heart Care

Full‑spectrum cardiac services from risk assessment to interventional procedures and rehabilitation.

Internal Medicine

Primary care, chronic disease management, and preventive screening for adults.

Pediatrics & Neonatology

Child‑friendly care for newborns, infants, and adolescents, including vaccinations and growth monitoring.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive women’s health services spanning prenatal care, fertility, and minimally invasive procedures.

Orthopedics & Sports Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment for bone, joint, and sports injuries, including rehabilitation programs.

Dermatology

Medical and cosmetic dermatology for skin, hair, and nail concerns, with evidence‑based treatments.

Diagnostics & support services

Our on‑site diagnostic center and support services are integrated into your care journey so you get faster answers and treatment.

Need help choosing the right service?

Our care coordination team can review your symptoms or previous reports and guide you to the most suitable department.
